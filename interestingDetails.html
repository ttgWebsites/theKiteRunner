Interesting Details

  • Despite being published only a decade ago (2003) it evolved as both a critically acclaimed and critically challenged novel very quickly. A movie version was even made just 4 years after the novel was published. The novel has landed on several national and international bestsellers lists.
  • Khaled Hosseini published another novel, A Thousand Splendid Suns, in 2007. This novel is not a sequel to The Kite Runner, however it focuses on similar topics and plights, this time following two Afghan women during essentially the same time period as his first novel. It also went on to be a best seller.
  • Hosseini is an Afghan born American citizen. He was born around the same time he set this novel, and experienced the tumultuous events that rocked his home country. However at much less of an extent than his protagonists, as he moved to Iran at a young age, and eventually moved to France and then America.
