Basic Info

  • Title
  • The Kite Runner
  • Author
  • Khaled Hosseini
  • Published
  • The novel was published in the United States by RiverHead Books in 2003.
  • Genre
  • Redemption, Blidungsroman.
  • Setting
  • The novel is set from 1975 through 2001 in both Kabul, Afghanistan and California.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is told in the first person from the protagonist's, Amir, point of view. He tells the story of his past bringing it concurrent with the present in the novel.
  • Conflict
  • Amir spends much of his life dealing with the guilt he feels for failing to help his friend Hassan who he witnessed being raped when they were boys. Amir spends nearly the entire novel trying to make up for his guilt.
