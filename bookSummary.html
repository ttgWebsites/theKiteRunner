Book Summary

  • So basically...
  • Amir, the protagonist, begins the story by retelling of an event that occurred in 1975 that would go on to permanently change his life. Amir does not give insight into what happens during this event, other than that it still haunts him, and it pertains to something that he had done to his friend Hassan. During the novel Amir takes the reader through his childhood experiences with his best friend Hassan as they try to survive through a trying time in their homeland of Afghanistan. The story is set during the fall of the Afghan monarchy to a Soviet invasion, and the rise of the Taliban regime.
